Lasagna recipe


Description

Lasagna is a rich, layered Italian dish made with pasta sheets, a savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and plenty of cheese. Baked until golden and bubbling, it’s the perfect comfort food — warm, hearty, and full of flavor in every bite. Whether for a family dinner or a cozy weekend meal, lasagna delivers satisfaction and a taste of home.
